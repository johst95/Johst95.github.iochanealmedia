About Chaneal

The Strategist Behind Memorable Events

Event Strategy Marketing Guest Experience Brand Activations Community Events

I'm Chaneal Conway, an events coordinator and marketing professional who works across hospitality, entertainment, retail, and community spaces. My specialty is restaurants — I understand the rhythms of service, the pressure of a big night, and what it takes to turn a dining concept into a destination. But great events follow the same principles in any industry: a clear strategy, smart promotion, and execution that doesn't drop the ball.

I specialize in helping brands and organizations create events that people actually show up for, talk about, and come back to. Whether it's a grand opening, a charity fundraiser, a pop-up activation, or a recurring themed night — I bring structure, creativity, and a calm hand under pressure to every project.

If you want events that are well-planned, well-promoted, and genuinely enjoyable to be part of — let's talk.

How It Works
